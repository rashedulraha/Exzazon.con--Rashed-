Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Find answers to common questions about our services

What is performance-based marketing?

Performance-based marketing is a model where agencies are compensated based on the results they deliver, such as leads generated, sales made, or other agreed-upon metrics. This aligns our interests with yours - we succeed when you succeed.

How long does it take to see results?

The timeline varies depending on your industry and goals, but most clients start seeing initial results within 30-60 days. Significant growth typically becomes apparent within 3-6 months of consistent effort.

Do you work with businesses outside of Rajshahi?

Yes, while we're based in Rajshahi, we work with businesses across Bangladesh and internationally. Our digital marketing services are not limited by geography.

What makes your agency different?

Our performance-based model sets us apart. We're invested in your success and structure our compensation around delivering measurable results. We also provide local market expertise combined with global digital marketing strategies.

How do you measure success?

We measure success through key performance indicators (KPIs) like conversion rates, cost per acquisition, return on ad spend, customer lifetime value, and overall revenue growth. We provide regular reports with detailed analytics.
